About Me

My name is Chris Donnelly and I am a junior at Menlo School in Atherton, California.
I am passionate about writing, data science, and the ways in which they can be combined.
I first started programming in the second grade when my sibling introduced me
to the Scratch programming language. Since then, I've used web development tools to edit
an educational tech resource for students in Nepal and data science techniques to engage
in astronomy research. I am also interested in writing and poetry in particular.
In my free time, I like to hike, spend time with my family, listen to music, run, and watch Netflix.
